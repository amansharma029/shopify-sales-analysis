# shopify-sales-analysis

 Shopify Sales Data Analysis Project

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing Shopify sales data to extract meaningful business insights. The goal is to understand sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends using Python and SQL.

---

## Dataset Information
The dataset contains e-commerce transaction data with the following columns:

- order_id – Unique ID for each order  
- customer_id – Unique customer identifier  
- product_id – Product identifier  
- product_price – Price of the product  
- discount_percent – Discount applied  
- quantity – Number of units purchased  
- shipping_cost – Cost of shipping  
- rating – Customer rating (1–5)  
- is_returned – Whether the order was returned (True/False)  
- discounted_price – Final price after discount  
- revenue – Total revenue generated  
- profit – Profit from each order

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
The following analyses were performed:

- Total revenue and profit calculation  
- Sales distribution across products  
- Impact of discounts on profit  
- Customer purchase behavior  
- Return rate analysis  
- Rating vs profitability relationship  

---

## 🔍 Key Business Insights
- High discount percentages reduce overall profit in many cases  
- A small number of products contribute to most revenue  
- Returns significantly impact profitability  
- Shipping cost affects final margin in low-priced orders  
- Customer ratings show moderate correlation with repeat purchases  

---

## 📌 Conclusion
